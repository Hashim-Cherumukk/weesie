The Challenge

Lumina Tech needed a brand identity that stood out in the crowded SaaS market. Their previous logo felt outdated and didn't communicate their core value: speed and clarity. The goal was to create a visual system that felt futuristic yet accessible.

The Solution

We developed a dynamic logo system based on light refractions. The color palette uses electric blues and deep slate to convey trust and energy. The typography is clean and Swiss-inspired, ensuring readability across digital and print mediums.
